# Life Calendar Wallpaper 🟠(Still in beta)

A minimalist Android wallpaper app that visualizes your year 
and life as a grid of dots on your lockscreen.

## Features
- Year Calendar — every day of the year as a dot
- Life Calendar — your entire life mapped in weeks
- Auto-refreshes at midnight every night
- Works on Android 8.0+

## Tech Stack
- Kotlin
- Canvas API
- WorkManager
- SharedPreferences
- MediaStore
